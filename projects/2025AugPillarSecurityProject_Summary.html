Upgrade Benefits

I wanted to share the current status of the cameras we have at Pillar and what we could upgrade to. This comparison highlights the limitations of our existing system and the advantages of upgrading to Unifi cameras.

The new technology would:

  • Extend camera range and improve night-time image capture
  • Add AI smart detection for enhanced monitoring
  • Include doorbell cameras tied directly to secretary phones, allowing both door unlock control and a live image of the person at the door

Currently, we have little real coverage inside the school. With Unifi cameras and their "hallway mode," we could easily achieve full coverage across all hallways and critical areas.
